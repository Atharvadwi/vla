How It Works

Our Design Process

I
Consultation

We discuss your requirements, plot details, and design preferences to understand your vision completely.

II
Briefing

A detailed project brief is created with measurements, reference drawings, and scope documentation.

III
Draft Design

Our CAD team produces the first draft of drawings for your review and initial feedback.

IV
Revisions

We refine and revise the drawings based on your feedback until they perfectly match your vision.

V
Delivery

Final drawings are delivered in DWG, DXF, and PDF formats — ready for construction or permit submission.
